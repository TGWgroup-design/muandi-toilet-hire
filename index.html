About Muandi Toilet Hire

Modern Sanitation. Reliable Delivery. Nationwide Reach.

Muandi Toilet Hire was founded to bring a modern, professional standard to the toilet and mobile fridge hire industry in South Africa. We believe temporary sanitation and cold storage shouldn't feel like an afterthought — it should be clean, reliable and easy to book.

Our mission is to deliver spotless, well-maintained VIP and standard toilets, plus dependable mobile fridges, to every site and event we serve — on time, every time.

Our vision is to become South Africa's most trusted mobile sanitation and rental partner for events, construction, mining, municipalities and private clients.
